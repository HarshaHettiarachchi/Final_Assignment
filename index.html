Avatar 3, Box Office Highlights (January 2026)

  • Avatar: Fire and Ash continues to perform strongly worldwide, having grossed over $1.3 billion globally so far, even as new films enter theaters. The movie has topped box office charts multiple weeks in a row and remains one of the top grossing films of 2025–26.

  • Despite strong earnings, Avatar: Fire and Ash has made less than the previous two Avatar films in North America, sparking industry analysis about whether changing audience trends or competition affected its performance.

  • After dominating the box office for weeks, the film is starting to slide down as several new releases gain traction — but it remains a major commercial success with a long theatrical run.

  • New releases are also shaking up the chart: Sci-fi action Mercy debuted strongly, taking the #1 spot on opening day while Avatar-related earnings continued in its sixth week.

  • Alongside Avatar, other films like 28 Years Later: The Bone Temple and Zootopia 2 are also competing strongly, showing that the global box office is active with both sequels and new genre entries.
