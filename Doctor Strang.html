A Journey Beyond Reality, Time, and the Self

The story centers on Dr. Stephen Strange, a world-famous neurosurgeon known for his extraordinary talent and equally large ego. Strange is highly skilled, confident, and arrogant, believing that his hands and mind make him superior to others. His life changes dramatically after a tragic car accident severely damages his hands. The injury ends his medical career and leaves him unable to perform surgery. This sudden fall from success to helplessness marks the emotional core of the film.

Unable to accept his fate, Strange spends all his money searching for a cure. His desperation leads him to Nepal, where he hears rumors of a mysterious place capable of healing the impossible. There, he discovers Kamar-Taj, a hidden world of magic and ancient knowledge. This moment shifts the film from a grounded medical drama into a full-scale mystical adventure, opening the door to realities beyond science.
